A Sanctuary of Calm

In the heart of the city, there exists a quiet corner where time seems to slow. Spica Studio was born from a simple desire: to create a space that breathes.

Here, the morning light filters softly through the leaves, dancing upon wooden tables. It is a refuge for the weary, a place where silence is cherished, and every detail invites you to pause.

Nature's Palette

We believe that beauty lies in the imperfect, the wild, and the natural. Our floral studio is not just a shop, but an artist's atelier.

Guided by the seasons, we gather textures and tones that speak of the earth. From the curve of a stem to the scent of dried eucalyptus, every arrangement tells a story of life, growth, and fleeting moments.

"Flowers are the music of the ground. From earth's lips spoken without sound."

The Art of Brewing

Coffee, for us, is a ritual. It is the warmth in your hands on a rainy day, the aroma that awakens memories.

We roast with care and brew with intention. Each cup is a crafted experience, designed to accompany conversations or solitary reflection. Paired with a simple bloom, it is our offering of comfort to you.

Gather & Create

Spica is more than a place; it is a community. We open our doors to dreamers, makers, and quiet souls.

Through our intimate workshops, we invite you to touch the soil, arrange the stems, and find joy in creating with your hands. In this shared silence, strangers become friends, bonded by the simple act of making something beautiful.

Come, stay a while.
